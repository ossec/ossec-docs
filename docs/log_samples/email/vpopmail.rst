Log samples from vpopmail and qmailtoaster 

In qmailtoaster vpopmail can be use for: pop3, pop3s, imap, imaps, smtp, submission and webmail.
Therefore each record can include respectively:
* vchkpw-pop3:
* vchkpw-pop3s:
* vchkpw-imap:
* vchkpw-imaps:
* vchkpw-smtp:
* vchkpw-submission:
* vchkpw-webmail:
